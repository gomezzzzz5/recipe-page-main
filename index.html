Simple Omelette Recipe

SAn easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. The classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lsrge eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meats herbs

Instructionss

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt until they are well mixed. you can add a table spoon of water or milk for a flutter texture
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter oil.
  3. cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling , pour eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surfacee.
  4. Add fillings {optional} When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of the omelete
  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide into a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and peppe if needed.
